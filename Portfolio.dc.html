About Me

I’m Manasi Lodha.

London, UK · Open to work

I’m a gameplay and XR developer based in London, happiest when I’m building interactive worlds that make people feel something.

Most of my work lives in Unreal Engine and Unity. I love the technical side, full-body inverse kinematics, AI-driven NPCs scripted in Yarn, XR interaction, haptics and spatial audio, but what really pulls me in is using all of it to tell a story or get a message across.

Right now I teach Unreal Engine and game development as a Lecturer at the University Campus of Esports in London. Before that I worked as a VR developer at ISRO and finished my MSc in Virtual Reality at Swansea University. Teaching has made me a sharper developer, you really learn a thing once you have to explain it clearly to someone else.

I’m always learning, and I’m steadily moving deeper into AR, mixed reality and digital twins. My goal is simple: work on projects that have real impact, and use immersive tech to create experiences that actually mean something.
